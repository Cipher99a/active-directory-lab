# Design Decisions

## Decision 001

### Title

Primary Virtualization Host

### Decision

The HP laptop (IT-ADMIN-01) was selected as the primary virtualization host.

### Reason

The laptop is available every day, allowing the enterprise lab to be used anywhere instead of only at home.

### Impact

- More frequent practice
- Portable lab
- Continuous learning
- Desktop reserved for future expansion

---

## Decision 002

### Title

Hybrid Enterprise Lab

### Decision

Use a hybrid environment consisting of physical devices and virtual machines.

### Reason

This architecture provides a more realistic enterprise environment while allowing the lab to remain portable.

### Impact

- Better troubleshooting experience
- Simulates a real company
- Supports future expansion
